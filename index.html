The following is a more rudimentary primer on the nuances of padding, margins, and borders. Div elements nested within each other, along with h5 elements. The surrounding div element is used to house the other elements.

From the FCC Section "Adjust the Margin of an Element": An element's margin controls the amount of space between an element's border and surrounding elements. Here, we can see that the green box and the red box are nested within the yellow box. Note that the red box has more margin than the green box, making it appear smaller. When you increase a box's margin, it will increase the distance between its border and surrounding elements.
